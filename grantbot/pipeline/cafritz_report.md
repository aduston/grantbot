# Grant Research Report: Morris and Gwendolyn Cafritz Foundation

## Overview

The Morris and Gwendolyn Cafritz Foundation offers grant opportunities that Ludlow-Taylor Elementary School's "Reading with Friends" program could potentially benefit from, given the foundation's focus on educational and community services initiatives within the Washington, DC metro area. Below is a detailed account of the grant criteria, application process, and additional considerations for their possible application.

## Grant Name and Link

- **Grant Name**: Morris and Gwendolyn Cafritz Foundation Grant
- **Grant Link**: [Morris and Gwendolyn Cafritz Foundation](https://www.cafritzfoundation.org/apply/funding-priorities/)

## Grant Amount

The specific grant amount is not mentioned. This suggests variability based on project needs or discretionary decisions by the foundation board.

## Eligibility Criteria

- The applicant must be an IRS-registered, tax-exempt 501(c)(3) organization with a public charity status of 509(a)(1) or 509(a)(2).
- The organization should serve residents in the District of Columbia, Maryland's Prince George’s or Montgomery Counties, or Virginia's Arlington or Fairfax Counties or the cities of Alexandria or Falls Church[^1^][^2^].

## Application Deadlines

Applications are accepted with deadlines on March 1, July 1, and November 1 at 5:00 p.m. ET annually[^3^].

## Additional Notes

- Application submissions must be made through the online grantee portal that opens one month before each deadline[^4^].
- The foundation does not require a Letter of Interest or Inquiry before submitting an application[^5^].
- Applications should be submitted annually, regardless of prior funding outcomes[^6^].

## Application Procedure

- Applicants must begin the process by creating an account on the online application portal[^7^].
- Reports and applications can be submitted online[^8^].
- Initial access to the application portal can be made via [this link](https://cafritzfoundation-portal.givingdata.com)[^9^].

## Considerations for Applicants

The foundation aligns with projects focusing on improving education and community services within the specified geographic areas, which aligns with "Reading with Friends" goals of offering literacy support to underserved students[^10^][^11^][^12^].
